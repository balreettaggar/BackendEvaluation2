Skin Care and Health

Dr. Mary Johnson

Skincare is essential for maintaining healthy and glowing skin. A good routine includes cleansing, exfoliating, moisturizing, and using sunscreen daily. Hydration and a balanced diet also play crucial roles. Choosing products with natural ingredients and suitable for your skin type helps prevent issues like acne, dryness, and premature aging.
